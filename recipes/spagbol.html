Odin's own Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

A heavenly dish eaten regularly, by the KING HIMSELF.Below you'll find a list of ingredients to make the dish. Can you make it to the perfection that the King would expect?

INGREDIENTS

  • Pasta (spaghetti or menstroni)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Basil
  • general spices
  • Mince meat (beef, chicken or pork)

How to make our Spaghetti Bolognese

  1. Cook pasta on a steady heated pan.
  2. Prepare meat, ensuring it is cleaned thoroughly before cooking.
  3. Add tomato sauce to pot. Stir until it settles.
  4. Finally, add the pasta, tomato sauce onto a plate, and enjoy!
